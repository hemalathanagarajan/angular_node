SERVICES WE DO OUR BEST

For complete peace of mind, your new Rolls-Royce comes complete with a four-year comprehensive ownership package, regardless of the motor car’s mileage* (Regional dealer warranty applies). This includes servicing, repairs and maintenance, as well as roadside assistance. To make servicing faster and more effective, every new Rolls-Royce features Condition Based Servicing. This means sensors in your Rolls-Royce actively monitor important components such as engine oil, brake pads and filters. If you’ve signed a ‘Get Connected’ form, this information will be transmitted to your dealership so that services can be scheduled to meet the precise needs of your vehicle, at a time most suited to you.
